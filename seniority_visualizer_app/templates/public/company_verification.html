How to Register

In order to register with the site, I'll first need to verify that you have active access to your company email. This is the only way that I can verify that you indeed work here and have access to the data the site uses already. When you provide a valid company email and prove that you aren't a bot, an email will be sent containing a link to register. That link will only be valid for a limited time. Once you verify via the email, you will be given the opportunity to provide an alternative email for general communication and administrative stuff like password resets. Eventually, a mailing list will be created that you can opt into that will let you know when the latest seniority data has been pushed into the site.

Be sure to check your spam folder since the mailer doesn't put out enough mail to gain a reputable status with most mailing systems!
