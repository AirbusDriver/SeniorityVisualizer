B6 Seniority Explorer

A pilot's one or more stop shop for visualizing seniority.

This is a project aimed at reviving, and expanding upon, the old seniority calculator. It can't, nor will it attempt to, provide advice about a pilot's career decisions. However, it can give some insight as to how we are doing as a pilot group, and how your place in it could look at any arbitrary point in time.

The information provided here is calculated under the assumption that each pilot will remain active on the seniority list until reaching their retirement date (age 65 rule). Some of the functionality may be inherently limited if you are new to the company and have not been added to the most recent seniority list publication. However, some "quick and dirty" calculations and estimations are available until a more accurate representation of your place in the list is available.
