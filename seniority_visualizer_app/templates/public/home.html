B6 Seniority Explorer

A pilot's one or more stop shop for visualizing seniority.

This is a project that aims to revive what was lost when the company's implementation of a seniority estimator was lost several years ago. It can't, nor will it attempt, to provide advice about a pilot's career decisions. However, it can give some insight as to how we are doing as a pilot group, and what your place in it could look at any arbitrary point in time.

The information provided here is calculated under the assumption that each pilot will remain active on the seniority list until reaching their retirement date (age 65 rule).
